About BLDRCODE Lab

BLDRCODE Lab was founded by an MIT Alumnus with a proven track record in Silicon Valley. We focus on practical skills, real-world projects, and mentorship to help you succeed. Every child should learn AI and computer science. There are many things that children will love about coding; creating their own game, apps, making their own animated stories and making a significant contribution to the building of today’s rapidly evolving technologies.
